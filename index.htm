About me

I am a postdoc at the University of Poiters with Enrica Floris. I did my PhD at the Departement of Mathematics and Computer Science of the University of Basel, under the supervision of Jérémy Blanc.

My main area of research is Birational Geometry; more specifically, I have worked on birational relations among Mori fibre spaces via the Sarkisov Program, with applications to Cremona groups.
